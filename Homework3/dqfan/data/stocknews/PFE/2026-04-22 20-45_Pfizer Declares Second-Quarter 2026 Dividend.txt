Board of Directors approves quarterly cash dividend of $0.43 per share
NEW YORK, April 22, 2026--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Pfizer Inc. (NYSE: PFE) today announced that its board of directors declared a $0.43 second-quarter 2026 dividend on the company’s common stock, payable June 12, 2026, to holders of the Common Stock of record at the close of business on May 8, 2026. The second-quarter 2026 cash dividend will be the 350th consecutive quarterly dividend paid by Pfizer.
