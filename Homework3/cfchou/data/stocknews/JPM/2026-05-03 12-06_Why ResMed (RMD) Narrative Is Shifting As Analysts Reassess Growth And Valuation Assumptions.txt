ResMed announced that long serving Chief Financial Officer Brett Sandercock plans to retire effective May 4, 2026, and will remain as an advisor to the CEO through the end of 2027. Aaron Bloomer has been appointed as the next Chief Financial Officer, joining from Exact Sciences with more than 17 years of global finance and operating leadership experience across Exact Sciences, Baxter International and 3M. ResMed is opening a new distribution center in Greenwood, Indiana, expected to begin operations in 2027 and serve as a major logistics hub supporting patients and providers across North America. The Greenwood facility is expected to employ more than 100 full time and contingent workers over time and, once fully operational, is planned to increase two day transit coverage across North America from 75% to 90% and shorten delivery times to Canada by one full day, based on company estimates.
