Title: Delta Air Lines Raises Worker Pay 4%
Date: 2026-05-01 19:54
URL: https://finance.yahoo.com/markets/stocks/articles/delta-air-lines-raises-worker-195407239.html?.tsrc=rss
Content:
This article first appeared onGuruFocus. Delta Air Lines (DAL,Financials) said it willboost pay for employees worldwide by 4%, investing approximately $500 million to workers even as increasing fuel prices weigh on the airline industry.Delta's fifth straight annual hike comes after a $1.3 billion profit-sharing payment to employees earlier this year. CEO Ed Bastian said the airline is continue to invest in people despite pressure from fuel prices and route delays.Delta said its fuel price projection for the June quarter was nearly $4.30 a gallon, double last year's level. The airline also said first-quarter fuel prices were $2.62 a gallon, over its plan.The increase reflects Delta's effort to maintain staff morale and service quality in a difficult cost environment.Investors will be watching to see whether rising labor and fuel costs would hurt margins in the coming quarters.
