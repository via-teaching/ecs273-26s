This mix of higher fair value estimates and trimmed targets reflects how analysts are weighing potential upside against execution and near term risks. JPMorgan keeps an Overweight rating on Tesco, signalling that the firm still sees the shares as attractive within its coverage, even with a lower price target.
Citi has raised its price target on Tesco in a separate research update, which supports the higher assessed fair value of £4.89 and points to continued interest from large global banks.
JPMorgan cut its Tesco price target to 480 GBp from 500 GBp, highlighting concerns around execution and shorter term risks, even as the broader stance on the stock remains positive.
The combination of a trimmed JPMorgan target and only modest fair value uplift suggests some analysts are cautious about how much upside is already reflected in the current valuation.
